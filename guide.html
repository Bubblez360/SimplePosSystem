Getting Started

Set up your store once, then start selling. Three steps and you're open for business.

Name your store

Go to Settings → Business Name. Add a logo too if you like — it shows on receipts and the app header.

Add your items

Open the Menu tab, tap +, and enter a name and price. Add variants (Small/Medium), add-ons, and a photo if needed.

Upload your GCash QR

Screenshot your QR from the GCash app, then upload it in Settings → GCash QR. It appears automatically when a customer pays with GCash.

💡

That's it — you're ready to sell. Everything you enter is saved on your phone instantly, with no account needed.

Installation

TindaPOS is a Progressive Web App — no app store, no download wait. Add it to your home screen and it runs like a native app, fully offline.

🤖Android (Chrome)
  1. Open the app link in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu, top-right.
  3. Tap “Add to Home screen” → Install.
  4. Open it from your home screen like any app.
🍎iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open the app link in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow).
  3. Scroll down → “Add to Home Screen”.
  4. Tap Add. Launch it from your home screen.
📶

After the first open, TindaPOS caches itself. From then on it launches and works with no internet — sync happens automatically when you're back online.

User Guide

The everyday flows, step by step.

Making a sale

Tap the items

In the Benta tab, tap each item the customer buys. The running total shows in the bar at the bottom.

Tap “Bayad na”

Choose Cash or GCash. For cash, enter the amount received and the change is calculated for you.

Confirm payment

For GCash, show the QR for the customer to scan, then tap “Nabayaran na”. A receipt appears.

Cancelling a sale

Changed their mind? On the receipt, tap Cancel Sale. For older sales, go to Ulat → Transactions, open the sale, and tap Cancel Sale. Stock is restored and the sale no longer counts in your reports — but it stays visible, marked Voided, so nothing is hidden.

➕

Want to add an item after paying? A completed sale can't be edited — just ring up a new sale for the extra, or cancel and re-ring it correctly.

Shifts & reports

Open a shift at the start of the day (enter your starting cash) and close it at the end to see a summary and expected cash. The Ulat tab shows sales by Today / Week / Year, best sellers, expenses, and profit — and exports to CSV with one tap.

Troubleshooting

If something's not working, start here.

You haven't uploaded one yet. Go to Settings → GCash QR and upload a screenshot of your QR from the GCash app.
Your sales are still safe on the phone. Sync retries automatically when the connection improves. Check that you're online and your Premium license is active in Settings.
Printing uses Web Serial, which works in Chrome on Android and desktop. Connect the printer, then tap Connect in Settings. iPhone doesn't support Web Serial — use on-screen receipts there.
Activation needs internet (once). Check the key for typos. If you lost it, use Retrieve key by email in Settings. Still stuck? Contact support below.
Fully close the app and reopen it so the new version loads. Your data is never affected by updates.
