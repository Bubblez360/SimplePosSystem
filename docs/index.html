⚙️ Getting Started

First-Time Setup

Before you start selling, configure your business info and payment options in the Settings tab (⚙️ bottom-right corner).

01
Enter your business name Your store name appears at the top of every screen and on digital receipts.
02
Upload your GCash QR code Screenshot your QR from the GCash app, upload it here. Use the crop tool to frame it perfectly.
03
Add your categories e.g. Drinks 🥤, Snacks 🍟, Milk Tea 🧋 — these appear as filter tabs while selling.
04
Add items to your menu Go to the Menu tab (📋) and tap the + button to add your first item.
💡

TindaPOS works fully offline — no internet required while selling. All data is stored on your device.

02
📋 Menu

Managing Your Menu

Go to the Menu tab (📋) to add, edit, or delete items. Everything you add here shows up instantly in the sell screen.

01
Tap the + button Opens an item form. Fill in the name and price (or leave price blank if using variants).
02
Add a photo or emoji Upload a product photo or pick an emoji. It shows as the item icon on the sell screen.
03
Add sizes / variants (optional) e.g. Small ₱65, Medium ₱75, Large ₱90. When variants exist, tapping the item opens a size picker.
04
Add add-ons (optional) e.g. Extra Pearls +₱10, Cream Cheese +₱15. These appear as tappable chips inside checkout.
05
Enable stock tracking (optional) Enter a stock count and toggle Track Stock on. It decrements automatically after each sale.

🛒 Selling

Taking an Order

The Sell tab (🛒) is your main work screen. Tap items to add them — the cart bar at the bottom tracks everything.

01
Filter by category Swipe the pill tabs at the top to quickly jump to Drinks, Food, Snacks, etc.
02
Tap an item to add it Items with sizes open a picker first. Plain items go straight into the cart.
03
Expand the cart Tap the total bar (▲) to see the full order list with big − and + buttons per item.
04
Tap "Bayad na →" to proceed Opens the checkout sheet with your complete order summary.

💡

The cart bar badge shows how many items are in the order. Expand it to edit quantities — the − button is large enough to tap easily even during a busy rush.

💳 Checkout

Checkout Process

The checkout sheet shows your full order, handles payment, and shows a digital receipt when done.

01
Review the order Tap add-on chips to include extras. Tap ✏️ to add a note per item (e.g. "less ice, no sugar").
02
Apply a discount (optional) Tap "🏷️ Diskwento". Toggle between flat ₱ or percentage %. The total updates live.
03
Choose Cash or GCash For Cash: enter amount received or tap a denomination chip. Change is calculated instantly.
04
Confirm payment For GCash: your QR shows full-screen for the customer to scan. Tap "Paid" when done.
05
Digital receipt appears A full receipt screen shows the order, total, and change. Tap "Done" to return to selling.

📊 Reports

Viewing Sales Reports

The Report tab (📊) shows your sales for today, this week, or this month — with a best sellers ranking and CSV export.

01
Switch time periods Tap Today / Linggo / Buwan tabs at the top to change the report range.
02
See revenue + Cash vs GCash split The summary shows total revenue and a side-by-side breakdown of payment methods.
03
Check Best Sellers A ranked list of your top items by quantity sold, with total revenue per item.
04
Tap a transaction to expand See the full itemized order, add-ons, notes, and any discount applied.
05
Export to CSV Tap "📥 Export CSV" in the header to download your sales data as a spreadsheet.
06
🕐 Shift

Shift Management

Track your opening cash float and get a full end-of-day summary when you close the shift.

01
Open a shift Settings → tap "Open Shift". Enter the starting cash in your drawer (e.g. ₱500 for change fund).
02
Sell normally throughout the day All transactions during the open shift are tracked automatically in the background.
03
Close the shift at end of day Tap "Close Shift" to see: cash sales, GCash sales, total, and expected cash in drawer.
💡

Expected Cash = Opening Float + Cash Sales. Count your actual drawer and compare to catch discrepancies.
